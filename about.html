About Sprout & Grow

Organic plants, expert guidance, and a commitment to sustainable gardening.

Who We Are

Sprout & Grow is a greenhouse nursery in Raleigh, North Carolina, focused on providing high-quality organic plants and practical support for gardeners of all experience levels. Our goal is to make sustainable gardening feel approachable, rewarding, and inspiring.

We believe that healthy gardens start with healthy plants, honest guidance, and thoughtful growing practices. That is why we carefully nurture our plants and encourage gardening methods that support environmental stewardship and long-term success.

Sprout & Grow serves home gardeners, urban growers, educators, and community groups looking for trusted resources and greener solutions. Whether you are planting your first herb garden or expanding an established outdoor space, our team is here to help you grow with confidence.

In addition to our plant selection, we value education and community engagement. Through workshops, resources, and personalized support, we aim to empower more people to build thriving organic gardens in their homes and neighborhoods.
